# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

LinguaMesh contributors, maintainers, and community members commit to a respectful, inclusive, and harassment-free environment regardless of background, identity, experience, ability, or viewpoint.

## Expected behavior

- Be considerate, specific, and constructive.
- Critique ideas and changes, not people.
- Respect privacy, security embargoes, and responsible-disclosure boundaries.
- Acknowledge mistakes, correct them, and accept reasonable maintainer decisions.
- Avoid harassment, threats, discrimination, sexualized conduct, deliberate intimidation, and publication of private information.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately through the maintainer contact route documented by the central `linguamesh-project` repository. Maintainers will review reports confidentially, avoid conflicts of interest, and may edit or remove contributions, issue warnings, restrict participation, or ban participants according to severity and recurrence.

This code applies in project spaces and when a person publicly represents the project. Retaliation against a reporter or participant in an investigation is prohibited.
